# User Stories

### Sign Up

As a new user, I want to sign up to view my elected officials and customize my feed to see who and what I want

### Sign In

As a registered user, I want to sign in to see the my customized feed

### Search

As anyone, I want to check for issues/people that are important to me and/or the complete opposite

### Home

As anyone, I want to see what is happening

### My Account

As a registered user, I want to change or add favorites/unfavorites to my feed
